About Me

Hi, I’m Sidney — a vocalist, songwriter, and actor based in Folkestone. I’ve spent years performing across the UK, working on TV and radio, collaborating with bands and artists, and developing my sound in studios and live settings. Music isn’t just something I do — it’s the world I live in every day.

I’ve trained with some of the UK’s leading vocal coaches, including Kate Cameron, Gareth Malone, Nicola Palmer, and ILĀ. Their guidance helped me develop a technique that’s expressive, flexible, and rooted in real artistry rather than rigid rules.

I now bring all of that experience into my teaching — whether that’s helping someone strengthen their voice, shape a song they’re writing, or record vocals in a way that finally captures how they feel when they sing.

What I Offer

  • Vocal coaching (technique, tone, texture, control, confidence)
  • Songwriting support (lyrics, melody, structure, creative flow)
  • Performance training (auditions, gigs, storytelling, presence)
  • Recording guidance (capturing vocals, phrasing, expression)
  • Production feedback & shaping your sound
  • Artist development — finding your voice, sound, and identity

How I Teach

My coaching style is warm, encouraging, and adaptable. I focus on clear, practical technique, tone and texture that feel personal to you, and creativity at the heart of everything. I’m not a “by the book” coach — I meet you exactly where you are.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you teach complete beginners?

Yes. Many of my students start with little or no formal experience. We’ll go at your pace, focusing on confidence, basics, and building a healthy relationship with your voice.

What styles of music do you teach?

I’m a multi-genre vocalist with a background in musical theatre, bands, and contemporary styles. We can explore anything from pop, indie, and rock to more theatrical or experimental approaches.

Where do lessons take place?

I teach from a private studio space in Folkestone with access to professional recording equipment. Online sessions may also be available on request.

Do you offer online lessons?

Yes, online lessons can be arranged if you’re not local or prefer working remotely. We’ll make sure the setup works for both of us so you still get clear, effective feedback.

Can you help with songwriting and recording?

Absolutely. I regularly work with students on their original songs, helping with lyrics, melody, structure, and performance. I also offer guidance on recording vocals and shaping your sound.

What is your cancellation policy?

I usually ask for at least 24 hours’ notice if you need to cancel or reschedule a session. This keeps the schedule fair and allows that slot to be offered to someone else.
