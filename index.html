About Me

Hi, I’m Sidney — a vocalist, songwriter, and actor based in Folkestone. I’ve spent years performing across the UK, working on TV and radio, collaborating with bands and artists, and developing my sound in studios and live settings. Music isn’t just something I do — it’s the world I live in every day.

I’ve trained with some of the UK’s leading vocal coaches – including Kate Cameron, Gareth Malone, Nicola Palmer and ILĀ – and their guidance helped me develop a technique that’s expressive, flexible, and rooted in real artistry rather than rigid rules.

I bring all of that experience into my teaching — whether that’s strengthening your voice, shaping a song you’re writing, or recording vocals in a way that finally feels like you.

  • Grade 8 vocalist with multi-genre experience
  • Background in musical theatre, bands & regular gigging
  • Private studio with professional recording equipment

FAQs

Answers to some of the most common questions.

Do I need any experience to start lessons?

Not at all. I work with complete beginners through to advanced singers. We’ll start from where you are and build from there at a pace that feels comfortable and encouraging.

Do you teach online as well as in person?

Yes. I offer both in-person sessions in Folkestone and online lessons via video call. Online lessons follow the same structure and are great if you’re further away or prefer working from home.

Can we work on my original songs as well as covers?

Absolutely. I love supporting songwriters. We can shape lyrics, melody and structure, as well as how you deliver the song vocally and emotionally.

Do you help with recording and studio confidence?

Yes. I have access to professional recording equipment and we can use sessions to practise studio technique, phrasing, mic control and building confidence in front of the microphone.

How do I pay for lessons?

Payment is usually made by bank transfer in advance of each session or package. Full details are provided when you book.

What is your cancellation policy?

I ask for at least 24 hours’ notice for cancellations or rescheduling where possible. With less than 24 hours’ notice, the full fee may still be payable, as it’s unlikely I’ll be able to offer the slot to another student.
