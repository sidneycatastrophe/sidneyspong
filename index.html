About Me

Hi, I’m Sidney — a vocalist, songwriter, and actor based in Folkestone. I’ve spent years performing across the UK, working on TV and radio, collaborating with bands and artists, and developing my sound in studios and onstage. Music isn’t just something I do — it’s the world I live in every day.

I’ve trained with leading vocal coaches including Kate Cameron, Gareth Malone, Nicola Palmer, and ILĀ. Their guidance helped me develop a technique that’s expressive, flexible, and rooted in real artistry rather than rigid rules.

I bring that experience into teaching — whether we’re strengthening your technique, shaping a song you’re writing, or recording vocals in a way that finally feels like “you”.

What I offer

  • Vocal coaching – technique, tone, texture, range & control
  • Songwriting support – lyrics, melody, structure, creative flow
  • Performance training – auditions, gigs, storytelling & stage presence
  • Recording guidance – capturing vocals, phrasing & expression
  • Artist development – finding your voice, sound & identity
