About me

Hi, I’m Sidney.

I’m a vocalist, songwriter and actor based in Folkestone. I’ve spent years performing across the UK – on stage, on TV and radio, and in bands and studio sessions. Music isn’t just something I do; it’s the world I live in every day.

I’ve trained with leading vocal coaches including Kate Cameron, Gareth Malone, Nicola Palmer and ILĀ. Their guidance helped me build a technique that’s expressive, flexible and rooted in real artistry, not rigid rules.

In lessons, I bring all of that experience into the room – whether we’re strengthening your voice, shaping a song, or preparing for a performance or studio session.

  • Grade 8 vocalist, multi-genre and texture-focused
  • Experience teaching all ages and levels
  • Private studio with pro recording equipment

Vocal coaching & artist development

What we can work on together

Sessions are tailored to you. We can stay purely with vocal technique, or weave in songwriting, performance and recording to build you as a complete artist.

Technique & tone

Breath support, vocal closure, mix, range, texture and stamina – all approached in a way that fits your voice, not a one-size-fits-all method.

Performance & confidence

Storytelling, stage presence, audition prep and managing nerves, with tools you can take into gigs, shows or studio sessions.

Songwriting & recording

Lyrics, melody, structure and arrangement, plus support for recording vocals so what you hear back finally matches how you feel.

FAQ

Questions people often ask

Do I need any experience to start?

No – complete beginners are very welcome. We’ll start from where you are, build healthy technique and confidence, and move at a pace that feels right for you.

What styles do you teach?

I specialise in rock, pop and contemporary styles, but I’m a multi-genre performer and open to all genres. The focus is always on what suits your voice and what you love to sing.

Where are lessons held?

I teach from a private studio space in Folkestone, with the option of online sessions if you’re further away or prefer to work from home.

Can we work on songwriting as well as singing?

Absolutely. We can combine vocal coaching with songwriting, arrangement and recording support so your songs and your voice grow together.

Can you help me prepare for an audition or recording?

Yes – we can focus sessions around auditions, gigs or studio dates, working on song choice, storytelling, performance and vocal consistency under pressure.
