About Me

Hi, I’m Sidney — a vocalist, songwriter, and actor based in Folkestone. I’ve spent years performing across the UK, working on TV and radio, collaborating with bands and artists, and developing my sound in studios and live settings. Music isn’t just something I do — it’s the world I live in every day.

I’ve trained with some of the UK’s leading vocal coaches, including Kate Cameron, Gareth Malone, Nicola Palmer, and ILĀ. Their guidance helped me develop a technique that’s expressive, flexible, and rooted in real artistry rather than rigid rules.

I now bring all of that experience into my teaching — whether that’s helping someone strengthen their voice, shape a song they’re writing, or record vocals in a way that finally captures how they feel when they sing.

What I offer

  • Vocal coaching – technique, tone, texture, control & confidence
  • Songwriting support – lyrics, melody, structure & creative flow
  • Performance training – auditions, gigs, storytelling & presence
  • Recording guidance – capturing vocals, phrasing & expression
